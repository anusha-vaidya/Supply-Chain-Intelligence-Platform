# Dashboard Storytelling

Each Power BI dashboard is designed to answer simple questions:

- Commodity: "Are prices and input costs moving in a way that increases risk?"
- Supplier & Logistics: "Which suppliers and routes are most likely to cause delays?"
- Inventory & Production: "Where are we at risk of stockouts or production disruption?"

Dashboards focus on clear KPIs, trends, and risk indicators rather than complex visuals.
