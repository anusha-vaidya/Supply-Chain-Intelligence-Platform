# Business Flow

This project follows the end-to-end supply chain flow:

1. Commodity markets influence steel input costs.
2. Purchase orders are placed with suppliers.
3. Shipments move through logistics with varying ETAs.
4. Inventory is consumed based on BOM and production plans.
5. Dashboards and alerts provide visibility and early warning.

Each module (commodity, supplier/logistics, inventory/production) is designed to reflect this real business flow.
